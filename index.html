Frequently asked questions

What type of businesses do you work with?

We specialise in small to medium-sized businesses across all industries. Whether you're a consulting firm, e-commerce store, marketing agency or service provider — if you have repetitive business processes, we can help automate them.

How long does it take to implement an AI solution?

Most projects take 2–4 weeks from initial audit to full implementation. Simple systems like follow-up emails can be live within a few days, while complex multi-step processes may take longer.

Do I need technical knowledge to use your systems?

Not at all. We build user-friendly systems that work with your existing tools. You'll receive training on how to monitor and manage them, but no coding or technical expertise is required.

What if the system doesn't work with my current software?

We integrate with most popular business tools — CRMs, email platforms, social media and more. During the audit we identify any compatibility issues and find solutions that work with your existing setup.

How much can I expect to save?

Our clients have saved anywhere from 5 to 40 hours per week on manual tasks. Depending on your hourly value, that can translate to $5,000+ in time savings monthly — plus improved accuracy and faster response times.

What happens if something goes wrong?

We provide ongoing support and monitoring. If any issues arise we fix them quickly, and all our systems include backup processes so nothing falls through the cracks.

Can I customise or change the system later?

Absolutely. As your business evolves we can modify, expand or add new systems — we build flexible workflows that grow with your needs.

How much do these systems cost?

Pricing varies with complexity and scope; we provide a custom quote after your free business audit. Most small businesses see positive ROI within the first month through time savings and improved efficiency.
